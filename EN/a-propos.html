Our approach

Our financial engineers support Partners at their clients' sites, whether with operational managers in the factory or with general management, thus combining field pragmatism and financial expertise.

They also have proven expertise in the field of public procurement, both in France and in the United Kingdom..

In addition to classic or tailor-made plans according to French accounting standards, our financial engineers are experienced in carrying out IAS/IFRS and FAS analyses, in designing and implementing framework contracts covering multiple deliveries.
